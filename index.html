About

The Jewelry brand was founded in 2018. Its founder, with whom it shares its name, driven by the innate love for the world of art and fashion, decided to leave his successful career as a hotel manager to follow a dream and face a new and passionate adventure. His creativity took shape through the hands of the historical and most renowned Mongolian jewelry artisans, who brought Jewelry's romantic and borderless imagination to life through their skills, telling his story through jewels that truly become works of art.
